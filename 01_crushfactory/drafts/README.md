# Crush Factory Legal Drafts

These files are store-submission drafts for Crush Factory.

They are not legal advice. Before publishing, replace every `TODO_*` placeholder, confirm the exact ad mediation partners and billing SDKs included in the release build, and have the final public pages reviewed for the target launch countries.

Public URLs:

- Privacy Policy: `https://pungpung22.github.io/game-policies/01_crushfactory/privacy/`
- Terms of Service: `https://pungpung22.github.io/game-policies/01_crushfactory/terms/`
- Support: `https://pungpung22.github.io/game-policies/01_crushfactory/support/`

Draft files:

- `privacy_policy.md`: user-facing privacy policy for Google Play and Apple App Store.
- `terms_of_service.md`: user-facing app terms.
- `store_privacy_disclosure_guide.md`: developer-facing answers for Google Play Data safety and Apple App Privacy.
- `sdk_data_inventory.md`: SDK and data inventory to keep policy labels aligned with builds.
