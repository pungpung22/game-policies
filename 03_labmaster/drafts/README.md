# Labmaster Legal Drafts

These files are store-submission drafts for Labmaster.

They are not legal advice. Before publishing, replace every `TODO_*` placeholder, confirm the exact AdMob, billing, analytics, crash reporting, and mediation SDKs included in the release build, and have the final public pages reviewed for the target launch countries.

Recommended public URLs:

- Privacy Policy: `https://pungpung22.github.io/game-policies/03_labmaster/privacy/`
- Terms of Service: `https://pungpung22.github.io/game-policies/03_labmaster/terms/`
- Support: `https://pungpung22.github.io/game-policies/03_labmaster/support/`

Draft files:

- `privacy_policy.md`: user-facing privacy policy for Google Play and Apple App Store.
- `terms_of_service.md`: user-facing app terms.
- `consent_flow.md`: implementation checklist for first-run Privacy Bay, Google UMP, and iOS ATT.
- `store_privacy_disclosure_guide.md`: developer-facing answers for Google Play Data safety and Apple App Privacy.
- `sdk_data_inventory.md`: SDK and data inventory to keep store labels aligned with builds.
