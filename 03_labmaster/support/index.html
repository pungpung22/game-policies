Support

For Labmaster support, privacy questions, purchase restore questions, or policy questions, use the public support issue tracker:

Open a Labmaster support request

What To Include

  • game name: Labmaster: Compute Lab
  • device model
  • Android or iOS version
  • app version
  • a short description of the issue
  • screenshots only if they do not include private information

Purchase Restore

If you need purchase help, first use the in-game Shop -> Purchase Restore button. Do not post payment card details, passwords, private personal information, or store account credentials.
