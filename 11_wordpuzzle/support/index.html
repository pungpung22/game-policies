Cipher Detective Support

For gameplay, save data, advertising rewards, purchases, privacy, or store questions, contact us by email or open a public support issue.

Email support · Open a GitHub support issue

Include with your request

  • App version and build number
  • Device model and Android or iOS version
  • Puzzle, screen, product, or ad reward involved
  • Steps that reproduce the issue
  • A screenshot that contains no personal or payment information

Saves and local data

Progress, ink, settings, and owned content are stored on the device. In-game Reset Progress preserves paid ink and owned paid content. Clearing the Game's operating-system storage or uninstalling removes the full local save. Record the problem before clearing storage because there is no Developer cloud save.

Ads and rewards

If a completed rewarded ad did not grant ink or a continue, report the reward type, approximate time, device, network state, and screen. Availability varies by consent, region, network, and ad inventory.

Purchases and restoration

Use Restore Purchases with the same store account for Detective Pass and eligible companion packs. Manage or cancel Detective Pass from the subscription section of your store account. Consumable Ink generally cannot be restored. If payment succeeded but the item was not delivered, do not buy it again. Email the product name, time, platform, and only the non-sensitive order reference needed to locate the transaction. Never send a card number, password, or complete receipt image.

Privacy and deletion

The Game has no account or Developer cloud save. See the Privacy Policy for local deletion and third-party provider choices.

Public issue warning

GitHub issues are public. Do not post your real name, email, phone number, address, password, card details, complete receipt, Apple ID, or Google account information. Use email for sensitive matters.
