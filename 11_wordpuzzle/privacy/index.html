Privacy Policy

Effective and last updated: July 14, 2026

pungpungstudi ("Developer," "we," or "us") provides the mobile game Cipher Detective: Word Puzzle (com.pungpungstudi.wordpuzzle, the "Game"). This policy explains how data is handled when you use the Game on Android or iOS.

Summary: The Game has no account system or Developer-operated cloud server. Progress and Ink are stored on your device. Google Mobile Ads may process device and ad interaction data. Apple or Google processes payments, and RevenueCat validates purchases and subscription access.

한국어 개인정보처리방침으로 이동

1. Scope and accounts

The Game does not require registration or login. We do not ask you to enter a name, email address, password, precise location, contacts, photos, camera data, microphone data, or payment-card details in the Game. Platform providers and third-party SDKs apply their own privacy terms to the services they operate.

2. Data stored on your device

The Game stores the following data locally so it can provide and restore gameplay:

  • Progress, solved puzzle identifiers, difficulty preference, endless-mode totals, and active puzzle state
  • Ink balance, hint use, unlocked companions, albums, skins, and equipped content
  • Language, accessibility, motion, and control settings
  • Detective Pass access, fulfilled product, transaction and renewal identifiers, and review-prompt state
  • Recent gameplay and commerce events held temporarily in app memory for diagnostics

This information is not uploaded to a Developer-operated server. Operating-system backup may copy app data according to your Apple or Google backup settings and their policies.

3. Advertising and consent

The Game uses Google Mobile Ads and Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP). Google and its advertising partners may automatically collect or share IP address and approximate location, device or account identifiers such as an advertising ID or app set ID, app launches and ad interactions, diagnostic information, and device, operating-system, app-version, and network information. These data may be used to provide and measure ads, limit frequency, prevent fraud, and diagnose service performance.

Where required, UMP asks for privacy choices before ads are requested. If Google requires a privacy-options entry point in your region, it is available from the Game's Settings. Ad personalization depends on your consent choices, region, device settings, and provider availability.

Rewarded ads appear only after you choose an advertised reward, such as ink or continuing a failed case. A native ad may appear inside the exit-confirmation screen. The current Game does not show automatic interstitial ads after solving a case or while you are actively solving a puzzle. An active Detective Pass removes non-optional ads, including the exit native ad; optional rewarded ads may remain available.

4. Purchases and restoration

In-app purchases are processed by Google Play or the Apple App Store. We do not receive your full payment-card information. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user identifier, product and transaction identifiers, purchase history, and subscription status to validate purchases, restore eligible purchases, and keep Detective Pass access current. The Game stores fulfillment and renewal identifiers locally to prevent duplicate Ink grants. Store providers and RevenueCat retain transaction data under their own policies and legal obligations.

5. Store review and platform features

After gameplay milestones, the Game may request an app review through the operating system's store-review interface. The store provider controls whether the prompt appears and handles any review or account data. The Game uses internet access for advertising, purchases, consent, and opening public policy or support pages.

6. Sharing and service providers

We do not sell information that the Game stores locally. Data may be processed by Google Mobile Ads and UMP for advertising and related purposes, by Apple App Store or Google Play for distribution, payments, restoration, and review features, and by RevenueCat for purchase validation, entitlement status, restoration, fraud prevention, and purchase diagnostics. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect users, rights, safety, or the integrity of the Game.

7. Retention, deletion, and your choices

Local data remains until you reset progress, clear the Game's storage, or uninstall it. The in-game reset preserves purchased ink and owned paid content so purchases are not accidentally lost; clearing the app's operating-system storage or uninstalling removes the complete local save. Because there is no Game account or Developer cloud save, we cannot remotely view, recover, or delete your local progress.

Data already processed by Google, Apple, Google Play, or RevenueCat is retained and deleted according to the relevant provider's policy and your provider-account controls. You may also reset or delete the Android advertising ID through device settings where available.

8. Children, security, and international processing

The Game is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us so we can assess the request.

Data sent to service providers is transmitted using the security mechanisms supplied by their SDKs, including encrypted transport where provided. No transmission or storage system is completely secure. Providers may process information in countries other than your own under their terms and applicable safeguards.

9. Policy changes

We may update this policy when the Game, SDKs, legal requirements, or platform rules change. We will update the date above and, when appropriate, provide an in-app or store notice.
