Privacy Policy

Effective and last updated: July 18, 2026

pungpungstudi ("Developer," "we," or "us") provides the mobile game Sync Bloom (com.pungpung.syncBloom, the "Game"). This policy explains how information is handled when you use the Game.

Summary: The Game has no account, advertising, analytics SDK, or Developer-operated server. Gameplay data is stored locally on your device. Apple processes App Store purchases.

한국어 · 日本語

1. Information you provide

The Game does not ask you to create an account or enter your name, email address, password, precise location, contacts, photos, camera or microphone data, or payment-card details.

2. Data stored on your device

The Game stores stage progress, flower ratings, move counts, selected garden and stone cosmetics, item and gold balances, language and accessibility settings, tutorial status, and identifiers for completed store transactions. This data is used to restore gameplay, preferences, purchases, and to prevent duplicate delivery. It is not uploaded to a Developer-operated server. Your operating-system backup may copy app data according to your Apple or Google backup settings.

3. Purchases

Optional purchases are processed by the Apple App Store. We do not receive your full payment-card information. Apple may process store account, payment, device, transaction, fraud-prevention, tax, and diagnostic information under Apple's policies. The Game receives product and transaction status needed to deliver purchased consumable gold and stores fulfillment identifiers locally.

4. Advertising, analytics, and tracking

The current Game does not include advertising, third-party analytics, social login, or cross-app tracking. It does not request Apple's App Tracking Transparency permission.

5. Retention and deletion

Local game data remains until you clear the Game's storage or uninstall it. Because the Game has no account or Developer cloud save, we cannot remotely view, recover, or delete your local data. Consumable purchase records held by Apple are retained and managed under Apple's policies and legal obligations.

6. Public policy and support pages

If you open our public policy or support pages, GitHub Pages may process IP address, browser, device, and access-log information under GitHub's privacy statement. The Game does not send your gameplay save to GitHub.

7. Children and security

The Game does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. Local data is protected by the security controls of your device. Store communications use the security provided by Apple and the operating system.

8. Changes and contact

We may update this policy when the Game, platform services, or legal requirements change. Questions may be sent to pung0805@gmail.com.
